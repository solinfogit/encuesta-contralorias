Auditoria General de la Republica

Brand Identity & UI Design System

Extracted from www.auditoria.gov.co — April 2026

1. Visual Tone & Overview

The AGR website projects an institutional, formal, and trustworthy visual identity. It follows Colombian government digital guidelines (GOV.CO) while maintaining its own strong blue-dominant brand identity. The design is clean, structured, and accessible.

Institutional & Formal

Government entity focused on fiscal control and auditing. The design conveys authority, seriousness, and professionalism through a structured layout and restrained color palette.

Blue-Dominant

Deep blues dominate the interface -- from the GOV.CO top bar through the navigation, headings, buttons, footer, and accent sections. Blue conveys trust, stability, and institutional authority.

Clean & Structured

White backgrounds with clearly separated sections. Content is organized in a traditional portal layout with a header, horizontal navigation, content cards, sidebars, and a footer.

GOV.CO Compliant

Follows the Colombian government digital identity: GOV.CO top bar in blue, government-standard accessibility patterns, and the official gov.co branding elements.

7. Key Visual Observations

Detailed notes on what was observed on the live AGR website.

Header Area

  • Top: GOV.CO institutional blue bar (#3366CC), centered "GOV.CO" text in white, ~42px height
  • Logo area: White background, logo on the left featuring the "A" mark with Colombian flag colors (yellow, blue, red) in a triangular/arrow shape
  • Logo text: "AUDITORIA" in bold dark text, "GENERAL DE LA REPUBLICA - COLOMBIA" smaller, "CONSOLIDANDO EL CONTROL FISCAL" as tagline in italics
  • Right side: Intranet and Correo icons in blue (#005CA6), search bar with bottom-border-only style (1px solid #005CA6)
  • Header separated from nav by a 5px solid #E7E7ED border

Navigation

  • Horizontal navigation bar on white background
  • Menu items in AGR Primary Blue (#0059A7), font-size 16px, weight 400
  • Dropdown items use very thin weight (100) at 15px in system blue (#0B5FFF)
  • Yellow/gold accent line (3px solid #FFC107) appears under the navigation bar
  • Six main menu items: Auditoria, Gestion misional, Gestion del Conocimiento, Atencion al Ciudadano, Transparencia, Participa

Content Area

  • Quick access cards row: 4 cards with rounded borders (#D0D2D3), centered logos and labels (Observatorio, SIA, Certificacion Contr., Revista)
  • Image carousel/slider with blue-themed banners
  • Section titles (NOTICIAS, ACCESOS DIRECTOS) are uppercase, underlined, in #0059A7, with icon prefix
  • Accesos Directos: Grid of blue bars (#0059A7) with white text, each ~106px tall (SGAS, Calendario, Biblioteca, Datos abiertos, Normatividad, Portal ninos)
  • News cards displayed in a list/grid format

Footer

  • Main footer: AGR Primary Blue (#0059A7) background, white text at 12.8px
  • Three-column layout: institutional info, contact details, social links
  • Bottom bar: Darker blue (#004884) with policy/sitemap links
  • Final bar: Near-black (#231F20) GOV.CO institutional footer
